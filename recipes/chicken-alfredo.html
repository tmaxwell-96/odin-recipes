High Protein Chicken Alfredo

High Protein and Low Calorie Alfredo That Tastes Great:

This is alfredo that tastes creamy but is higher in protein and lower in calorie. It tastes great and it's easy to make, I suggest you try it!

Makes one portion.

Ingredients

  • 150g chicken breast
  • 100g Pasta of choice
  • One third of an onion
  • 166 mL cottage cheese
  • 0.33 cup skim milk
  • 9.3g of grated parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Cut chicken into bite sized pieces.
  2. Mix with spices, cook on high head until internal temperature is 165 degrees F. Set aside.
  3. While cooking chicken, cook pasta. Set aside when done.
  4. Dice onion,
  5. Add cottage cheese, diced onion, skim milk, grated parmesan, and spices to blender cup. Blend until Creamy.
  6. Add sauce to chicken pan with a teaspoon of corn starch. /li>
  7. When sauce thickened, add chicken back and add pasta. Simmer for 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
